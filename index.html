How It Works

1

Install MetaMask

Download the MetaMask extension from the official website or your browser's app store. It's available for Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.

2

Click Connect

Visit a Web3 app and click the "Connect Wallet" button. The app will request permission to access your wallet information.

3

Confirm in Wallet

A MetaMask popup will appear. Review the permission request and click "Connect" to establish the connection securely.

Your Private Keys Stay Safe

MetaMask never shares your private keys. Apps only get read access to your address.

What Is Web3?

Web3 enables decentralized apps powered by blockchain. Wallets like MetaMask are your identity and gateway.
